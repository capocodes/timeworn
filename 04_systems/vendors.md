# Vendors — intent

**Purpose:** **Buy / sell** economy between and around raids. **Not** slice v1 — see [Playable Slice — Strip Order](../05_production/playable_slice_strip_order.md).

## Intent

- **Vendors** offer **gear, consumables, materials, repairs**, and possibly **contracts** or **faction rep** gates.
- **Primary home:** **base / social** layer ([Meta Loop](../01_vision/meta_loop_intent.md)). **In-raid** vendors or **black market** encounters are **optional** later.
- **Currency / barter** — TBD (cash, scrap, faction tokens, item-for-item).

## Links

- **Crafting** often **consumes** vendor-bought inputs — [Crafting](crafting.md).
- **Skills** may **unlock** vendor tiers or prices — [Skills](skills.md).
- **Loadout** ([Core Session Loop](../01_vision/core_extraction_loop.md)) is fed by what vendors and crafting produce.
