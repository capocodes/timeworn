# Skills — intent

**Purpose:** **Progression** beyond raw **loot power** — perks, proficiencies, survival, craft/vendor lines. **Not** slice v1.

## Intent

- **Examples (non-exhaustive):** weapon familiarity, reload stability, carry capacity, craft efficiency, vendor discounts, faction dialogue.
- **Open:** **Account-wide** vs **per-character**; interaction with **seasons / wipes** (`../01_vision/meta_loop_intent.md`).
- **Solo vs co-op quests** may award **different skill profiles** (better co-op rewards vs challenging solo — same meta doc).

## Balance

- Gaps must not make **new players** non-viable in **duo/trio** extraction — see **anti-grief** and TTK direction in `../01_vision/design_goals.md`.
