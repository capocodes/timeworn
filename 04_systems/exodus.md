# Exodus *(stub)*

**Purpose:** The **endgame craftable objective**. Exodus is what the entire build → defend → extract spine points toward. Building it is the win condition for a full season/progression arc.

## Fantasy

You're not just surviving — you're **building your way out**. Exodus is a large-scale craftable (vehicle? machine? portal?) that requires sustained investment across multiple raids: rare materials, defended infrastructure, and completed tech-tree prerequisites. Completing it triggers the **Final Extract** — the ultimate version of the extraction mechanic.

## Mechanics

- **Construction:** Built across multiple raids at a **persistent build site** (town claim or dedicated location).
- **Requirements:** Rare materials from all three loot tiers, tech building outputs, and specific [Crafting](crafting.md) recipes.
- **Vulnerability:** While under construction, Exodus is a **raid target** — other players and AI can attack it. Ties directly to the [Building](building.md) defense loop (turrets, walls, traps).
- **Completion:** Finishing Exodus triggers the **Final Extract** ([Extraction](extraction.md) tier 3 / special train), ending the player's progression arc for that season.
- **Seasonal tie:** Exodus completion may feed into [Stats](services.md) (seasonal leaderboard) and [Resets & World Events](../01_vision/resets_and_world_events.md) (season end state).
