# Enemies — future robots (PvE AI)

**Purpose:** Define **who you fight** when the threat is **AI** — not slow, readable **human** mobs. Ties **fear**, **movement**, and **loot fantasy** together.

## Why not “people” as primary PvE?

- **Biped / “dude with a gun”** enemies read as **familiar** and often **flat** for horror/tension — especially **slow** human movement.
- **Project direction:** PvE pressure should feel **alien, wrong, and dangerous** — aligned with `../arizona_game_concept.md` (unnatural mechanical presence in the frontier).

## What *is* scary (reference touchstone)

- ***Arc Raiders*** — example of an enemy that **lands** despite (or because of) **readable** patterns: **attacks are slow to commit but highly lethal** when they connect, while the unit has **fast repositioning in all directions**. That **gap** between **movement** and **strike** creates **PvE tension** better than **slow humans**.
- **Not a copy** — steal the **principle**: **mobility + telegraphed lethality**, not one specific boss moveset.

## Canon: all PvE AI = future robots

- **Lore:** Hostile units are **robots / machines sent from the future** (variants: scouts, bruisers, drones, etc. — **TBD** roster).
- **Human** conflict (**Apache**, **Cowboys**, players) stays in **faction / PvP** space; **AI slot** is **machines** so encounters stay **distinct** from “another posse.”
- **In-world faction name** for the machines — still **TBD** (avoid generic “bots” in shipping copy) — see `../01_vision/meta_loop_intent.md`.

## Loot → progression loop

- **Salvaging / looting** destroyed or disabled **robot** units yields **future-tech** components.
- Those parts **feed crafting** of **futuristic weapons** and gear (`crafting.md`) — explicit bridge from **PvE risk** to **power fantasy** (period musket → tech escalation is a **deliberate** arc). **Fully future** **base weapons** live only at **yellow** legendary (`weapons_loot_tiers.md`).
- **Slice v1** may still focus on **musket + first robot type**; full **tech tree** post-slice.

## Design guardrails

- **Variety** through **silhouette, behavior, and weaponized role** — not “human reskin.”
- **Audio / VFX** sell **wrong-tech** in a Western frame.
- **TTK / pacing** still obeys `../01_vision/design_goals.md` (methodical, mid-first) — robots can be **fast** without turning the game into a **twitch** arena.
