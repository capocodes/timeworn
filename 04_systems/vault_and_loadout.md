# Bank vault & loadout

**Purpose:** Split **what you bring into a raid** from **what you keep long-term** across **hub resets**.

## Definitions

| | |
|--|--|
| **Loadout** | Items on your **person** (and immediate raid carry) when you **deploy** — **tighter** capacity; defines what you **risk** in the raid. |
| **Bank vault** | **Larger** persistent storage than loadout — **off-raid** account (or character) inventory. |

## Persistence vs weekly hub wipe

- **Weekly reset** (**`../01_vision/resets_and_world_events.md`**): **Homestead / base build** is **wiped**. Contents of the **bank vault** **persist** — you **keep** everything stored there.
- **Rationale:** Rebuild fantasy without **zeroing** long-term **loot** progress; vault is the **safety** layer for **extracted** gear and mats.

## Flow (target)

- **Before raid:** Pull from **vault** into **loadout** (subject to loadout limits).
- **After extract / round review:** Surviving loot can be **stashed** into **vault** (rules, fees, sorting — TBD).
- **Lost on death / failed extract:** Typically **loadout + carried raid loot** per raid rules; **vault** unaffected unless a **special** event says otherwise.

## Other cadences

- **Seasonal** or **world events** *might* touch **vault** rules (e.g. seasonal wipe, taxed migration) — **not** locked; default assumption is **weekly** only clears **hub build**, **vault** stays unless **season** doc says otherwise.
