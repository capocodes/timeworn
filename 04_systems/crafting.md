# Crafting — intent

**Purpose:** Turn **loot + parts** into **items, consumables, base modules, quest turn-ins**. **Not** slice v1.

## Intent

- **Inputs:** **raid loot**, **vendor** purchases, **skill-gated** recipes (see `skills.md`, `vendors.md`). **Robot salvage** from PvE machines (`enemies_future_robots.md`) gates **futuristic** weapons and tech. **Loot tiers** for guns + **attachment** catalog: `weapons_loot_tiers.md`, `attachments_catalog.md`.
- **Where:** Mostly **between raids** at **base** benches/stations. **In-raid field craft** only if it supports fantasy without scope explosion.
- **Depth** — TBD: recipe discovery, tiers, durability, time-gates. Align with **slow, methodical** combat (`../01_vision/design_goals.md`) so crafting isn’t an unpaid second job unless you want that identity.
