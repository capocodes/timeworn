# Crafting — intent

**Purpose:** Turn **loot + parts** into **items, consumables, base modules, quest turn-ins**. **Not** slice v1.

## Intent

- **Inputs:** **raid loot**, **vendor** purchases, **skill-gated** recipes (see [Skills](skills.md), [Vendors](vendors.md)). **Robot salvage** from PvE machines ([Enemies — Future Robots](enemies_future_robots.md)) gates **futuristic** weapons and tech. **Loot tiers** for guns + **attachment** catalog: [Weapons & Loot Tiers](weapons_loot_tiers.md), [Attachments Catalog](attachments_catalog.md).
- **Where:** Mostly **between raids** at **base** benches/stations. **In-raid field craft** only if it supports fantasy without scope explosion.
- **Depth** — TBD: recipe discovery, tiers, durability, time-gates. Align with **slow, methodical** combat ([Design Goals](../01_vision/design_goals.md)) so crafting isn't an unpaid second job unless you want that identity.
