# Base building — intent *(stub)*

**Purpose:** Between-raid **homestead / neighborhood** construction — **PvE-only** social space (`../01_vision/meta_loop_intent.md`). **Full mechanics TBD**; this file anchors **direction** until you spec modules, snap, limits, and UI. **In-raid** **defenses** and **town → tech → Exodus** spine are **`../01_vision/game_loop.md`** (action sandbox), not duplicated here.

## Inspiration *(touchstone)*

- **Building feel** is **inspired by** ***Dune*** — especially ***Dune: Awakening***-style **settlement / construction** fantasy (placement, modules, surviving the cycle — **not** a licensed copy of their systems).
- Aligns with **weekly hub reset** (`../01_vision/resets_and_world_events.md`): **build** is **wiped** on the tick; **bank vault** keeps **loot** (`vault_and_loadout.md`).

## Open (to flesh out later)

- **Pieces:** foundations, walls, utilities, crafting stations, cosmetics, shared-plot rules with friends.
- **Costs:** **raid loot**, **vendor** mats, **crafting** outputs (`crafting.md`).
- **Neighborhood shards** — plot assignment, caps, portable/migrate (`player_server_cardinality.md`).
