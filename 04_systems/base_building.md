# Base building — intent *(stub)*

**Purpose:** Between-raid **homestead / neighborhood** construction — **PvE-only** social space ([Meta Loop](../01_vision/meta_loop_intent.md)). **Full mechanics TBD**; this file anchors **direction** until you spec modules, snap, limits, and UI. **In-raid** **defenses** and **town → tech → Exodus** spine are in the [Game Loop](../01_vision/game_loop.md) (action sandbox), not duplicated here.

## Inspiration *(touchstone)*

- **Building feel** is **inspired by** ***Dune*** — especially ***Dune: Awakening***-style **settlement / construction** fantasy (placement, modules, surviving the cycle — **not** a licensed copy of their systems).
- Aligns with **weekly hub reset** ([Resets & World Events](../01_vision/resets_and_world_events.md)): **build** is **wiped** on the tick; **bank vault** keeps **loot** ([Vault & Loadout](vault_and_loadout.md)).

## Open (to flesh out later)

- **Pieces:** foundations, walls, utilities, crafting stations, cosmetics, shared-plot rules with friends.
- **Costs:** **raid loot**, **vendor** mats, **crafting** outputs ([Crafting](crafting.md)).
- **Neighborhood shards** — plot assignment, caps, portable/migrate ([Player ↔ Server Cardinality](player_server_cardinality.md)).
