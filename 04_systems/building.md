# Building — in-raid *(stub)*

**Purpose:** The **in-session** build loop from the [Game Loop](../01_vision/game_loop.md) — town claiming, placing defenses, constructing tech buildings. Distinct from [Base Building](base_building.md) (between-raid homestead / social space).

## Intent

- **Tier 1:** Workbench, blacksmith, **town claim** — staking ground to transition into tier 2.
- **Tier 2:** **Town → tech buildings → defenses** — the RTS-flavored spine; defenses protect what you've built and unlock **Exodus**.
- **Tier 3:** Outputs of the build chain feed the **Exodus** craftable (endgame objective).
- **PvPvE pressure:** Other players and bots can contest / destroy structures — "holding ground" matters.
