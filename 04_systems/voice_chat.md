# Voice chat *(stub)*

**Purpose:** In-game voice communication for **party** and optionally **proximity** channels.

## Intent

- **Party channel:** Always-on for duo/trio members in the same raid.
- **Proximity channel (TBD):** Hear nearby players (friend or foe) — adds tension in PvPvE but has grief / info-leak risk. Align with [Design Goals](../01_vision/design_goals.md) anti-grief stance.
- **Mute / volume:** Per-player controls.

## Open

- [ ] Provider (Vivox, Steam Voice, Epic Voice, custom).
- [ ] Proximity vs party-only (or both with toggle).
- [ ] Push-to-talk vs open mic default.
- [ ] Integration with [Multiplayer](multiplayer.md) session lifecycle.
