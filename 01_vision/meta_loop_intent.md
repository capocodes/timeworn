# Meta loop — intent (between / around raids)

**Purpose:** Capture **longer-arc** direction so extraction raids don’t exist in a vacuum. **Not** a commitment to ship order—**intent** only until scoped.

## 1. Quests — co-op vs solo

- **Co-op quests** — **Better rewards** for grouping (risk/reward tuned to duo/trio).
- **Solo quests** — **Super fun and challenging** on their own; not “worse,” different **profile** (e.g. narrative, difficulty, self-reliance).

*Details: structure, daily/weekly, narrative tie-in — TBD.*

## 2. Base building & social (PvE-only enclave)

- **Systems doc (stub):** `../04_systems/base_building.md` — mechanics **TBD**; **inspired by** *Dune* / *Dune: Awakening*-style building (**not** a copy).
- **Almost a sub-game:** persistent or session-based **home** space where players **spend time** between raids.
- **Loot sink:** use **raid loot** to **build out** a **base** (recipes, modules, cosmetics — TBD). If **neighborhood shards** are used (`../04_systems/player_server_cardinality.md`), **portable / migratable** homesteads may be required to **sunset** quiet hubs without wiping progress.
- **No PvP in base** — **PvE / social only**; conflict stays in **raid spaces** (aligns with “no safe zones” **in raid**; base is **explicit exception** for chill/craft/social—reconcile copy carefully with `design_goals.md`).
- **Shared bases** — **Friends** can **collaborate** on a shared build / hangout loop.

*Conflict with “no safe zones” globally: treat **raid maps** as always dangerous; **base** is a **designated** non-PvP layer unless we revise the pillar.*

### Weekly base / hub reset *(under consideration)*

- **Idea:** **~Weekly** reset of **hub / homestead build** state — **Dune-inspired** rhythm (periodic pressure + **fresh claim** on your plot); full **multi-timeline** stack (**seasonal**, **world events**) lives in **`resets_and_world_events.md`**.
- **Why it’s attractive:** **Rebalance** boundary, **rebuild** challenge, **neighborhood ops** (tune shard count **next week** — `../04_systems/player_server_cardinality.md`).
- **What survives weekly wipe:** **`Bank vault`** (bigger than loadout) **persists**; **base** does not — `../04_systems/vault_and_loadout.md`. **Risks** — `resets_and_world_events.md`.
